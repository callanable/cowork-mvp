Sign in once. Every tool ready on day one.

An installable, governable AI skills marketplace for Bloomerang. Sign in with Google → the platform reads your identity and provisions the right MCPs and skills for your role. Skills compose the MCPs your org already owns into one-command workflows.

Sign-in is real Google OIDC. The role-to-MCP mapping is mocked here for the demo; in production it's driven by Workspace groups, SCIM, or HRIS — owned by the platform's identity broker.

Source: cowork-mvp (marketplace + skills) and bloomerang-mcp (customer-data primitive).

Your provisioned access
The role above is a demo selector. In production, it's derived from your identity claims — Google Workspace group memberships, SCIM-synced HRIS attributes, or platform-owned role assignments. Each MCP's wiring is governed at the platform layer; skills are portable across roles and inherit whichever data primitives are scoped to the user. See DESIGN.md for the full framing.
